Water tourism refers to the use of water-based activities as a form of tourism. This can include activities such as boating, fishing, kayaking, snorkeling, and more.

There are many destinations around the world that offer water tourism, including several options for travelers from India. Some popular options include:

The Maldives:

The Maldives is an archipelago of 26 coral atolls located in the Indian Ocean. It is known for its crystal clear waters and beautiful white sandy beaches, making it a popular destination for water-based activities such as snorkeling, diving, and whale watching. Prices for water tourism in the Maldives can vary, but generally start at around INR 10,000 per person for a half-day snorkeling trip and go up to around INR 60,000 per person for a multi-day diving package.

Thailand:

Thailand is another popular destination for water tourism, with options such as kayaking, snorkeling, and diving available in destinations such as Koh Tao and Koh Samui. Prices for these activities can range from around INR 1,000 per person for a half-day kayaking trip to around INR 20,000 per person for a multi-day diving package.

Seychelles:

The Seychelles is an archipelago of 115 islands located in the Indian Ocean. It is known for its stunning beaches, clear waters, and rich marine life, making it a popular destination for water-based activities such as snorkeling and diving. Prices for these activities in the Seychelles can vary, but generally start at around INR 5,000 per person for a half-day snorkeling trip and go up to around INR 40,000 per person for a multi-day diving package.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands:

Located in the Bay of Bengal, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands are a popular destination for water-based activities such as snorkeling, diving, and kayaking. Prices for these activities can range from around INR 1,000 per person for a half-day snorkeling trip to around INR 20,000 per person for a multi-day diving package.

These are just a few examples of destinations that offer water tourism and the approximate cost of these activities. Prices can vary depending on the specific activity, duration, and location.
